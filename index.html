BTS is a South Korean boy band formed in 2013 by Big Hit Entertainment. The team consists of seven members: RM (the leader of the group), Chin, Shugi, J-Hope, Jimin, Vi and Jongguk[1]. In July 2017, the band changed the official logo and the transcript of their name in English, as a result of which they became Off-stage.
